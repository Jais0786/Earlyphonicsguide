A Logical Approach to Early Reading

Early Phonics Guide was created to help parents navigate the chaotic early stages of reading development. When children guess words, the issue is often related to foundational blending and listening skills.

Instead of pushing harder practice, we focus on introducing sounds, listening activities, and blending in a structured, screen-free environment.
